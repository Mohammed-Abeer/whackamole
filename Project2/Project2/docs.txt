            HOW TO PLAY
1. There are holes from where moths will come and you have to hit them with hammer.
2. Hitting the moths in right time will give you 1 point. There is 120 seconds of play time.
3. There are three levels according to their respective difficulties. 
4. In case of multiplayer, one opponent will use mouse and another keyboard. Together beat the moths.
5. Hitting evil moth will take your life.
